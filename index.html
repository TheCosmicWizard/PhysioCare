FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is home physiotherapy safe?
Yes, absolutely. Our therapist is BPT/MPT certified and follows all clinical safety protocols during home visits. We bring sterile gloves and equipment when required, and conduct a full safety assessment before beginning any treatment.
How do online physiotherapy sessions work?
Sessions are conducted via Google Meet or Zoom. You'll receive a link after booking. The therapist will visually assess your movement and posture, guide you through exercises in real time, and create a take-home exercise program.
Which areas in Pune do you cover for home visits?
We cover most areas in Pune including Kothrud, Baner, Wakad, Aundh, Pimple Saudagar, Viman Nagar, Hadapsar, Kondhwa, Deccan, Shivajinagar, Kharadi, and nearby localities. Contact us for your specific area.
How many sessions will I need?
It varies by condition. Acute injuries may resolve in 4–6 sessions. Chronic conditions or post-surgery rehab typically require 12–24 sessions over several weeks. After your first assessment, we'll give you a clear timeline and expectations.
Do I need a doctor's referral to book a session?
No referral is needed for most conditions. You can book directly. However, if you have a recent surgical history or serious medical condition, bringing your discharge summary or prescription helps us plan the safest treatment for you.
What should I prepare for a home visit?
Just wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing and clear a small floor space (about 6x4 ft) if possible. Have any relevant medical reports or X-rays ready. The therapist brings all necessary equipment — you don't need to purchase anything.
